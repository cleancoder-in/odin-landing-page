Explore HIMACHAL

Spectactular snow capped mountains, lush green forests, gushing rivers and streams.

Most popular places

SHIMLA, capital town of Himachal and famous for lush greenery, pleasant climate, and snow-clad hills.

MANALI, is known for its unlimited adventure opportunities, the famous Hadimba Temple, the scenic Rohtang Pass, the snow-laden Solang Valley and its delightful culinary scene.

DHARAMSHALA, is famous for stunning natural beauty, Buddhist monasteries, and vibrant culture. Dharamshala Cricket Stadium is a must-visit for cricket fanatics.

SPITI, is famous for its natural beauty and cultural heritage. Tabo monastery is one of the most prominent attractions in Spiti.
